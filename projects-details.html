Collection of Vietnamese Folksongs

A curated collection of Vietnamese folksongs, exploring traditional melodies of Vietnam and their cultural significance. This is an 18-minute 5-movement suite for wind ensemble that I started in my junior year of high school. Using folksongs as the base of the melodies, I arranged 10 of them using inspiration from other types of Vietnamese music, like Tet music, Vietnamese bolero, and temple music. This was a long project, and I never would have guessed that it would have led me all over Vietnam, but it was always a work of passion. Collection of Vietnamese Folksongs
